Ingredients:
 - A Banana, papaya, or apple
 - 400ml of cold milk
 - Any amount of sugar

Directions:
 1. Cut the fruit into small pieces and put it at a blender or
    food processing recipient.

 2. Add the milk and the sugar, and blend it until the mixture
    becomes uniform.

 3. Serve and drink.

Tip:
 1. If you want to make it more or less consistent, just change
    the proportion of the fruits or milk slightly.
